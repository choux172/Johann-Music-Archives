Archive Timeline

Milestones in Our Collection

1723

Bach Arrives in Leipzig

Our earliest manuscripts date from Bach's appointment as Thomaskantor, including cantata drafts and organ tablatures in his own hand.

1791

The Mozart Requiem Sketches

Fragmented pages from the final, unfinished Requiem in D minor, acquired through the Constanze Weber estate papers.

1876

First Bayreuth Festival Scores

Orchestral parts and conductor markings from the inaugural Ring cycle performance at the Bayreuth Festspielhaus.

1913

The Rite of Spring Premiere

Annotated rehearsal scores from the legendary Paris premiere of Stravinsky's groundbreaking ballet, including Nijinsky's choreographic notes.

2024

Digital Preservation Initiative

High-resolution scanning of the complete manuscript collection begins, making the archive accessible to researchers worldwide.
